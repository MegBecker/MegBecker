Trust Your Instinct

Following the creation of the Reflection of Practice, I was challenged to summarize my experience into a single phrase. The extensive process of editorial revision and struggle of self-doubt that characterized my semester led to this product. Twenty frames are printed and packaged, allowing the user to create multiple compositions using the squares and layers and illustrating process: creating, recreating, pushing too far and not pushing enough, and eventually trusting our own instincts to create uniquely.
