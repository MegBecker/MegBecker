The Letter Deck

A card deck created to counteract recent trends of using only free fonts and a misunderstanding of what makes a good typeface. The traditonal card suits have been replaced with Serif, Sans Serif, Display, and Script, allowing the cards to be used as a traditional deck as well as for type pairing and classification, interesting designers and nondesigners alike.
