Adventures Through Type & Space

An early-90's-inspired video game that takes a humerous and nostalgic approach to educating about typography. The player moves backwards through typographic history with the task to defeat a rogue pirate, restore order, and gain the skills necessary to become a master of type. Target audience is kids, kids at heart, and those passionate about type who need a good laugh.

This is a collaboration with Miles Elliott. While I have primarily worked on the illustrations and he has primarily focused on the code and interface design, the entire effort has been equal between storyboarding, character design, and wording.
