Adventures Through Type and Space

A video game that takes the player backwards through typographic history with the task to defeat a rogue pirate, restore order, and gain the skills necessary to become a master of type. The player will collect items and interact with typographers throughout their journey in towns representing famous faces.

Collaborator: Miles Elliott.
