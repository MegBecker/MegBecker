An Exploration of Fear

At a time when I was faced with fears both personal and of the photo studio, I began this project to force myself to face them. I started by interviewing each friend and getting to the root of their deepest fear and turning it into a physical metaphor, keeping it vague for viewers to project their thoughts into the images. The process quickly became a discovery of how universal yet deeply personal each of our individual fears are and the tragedy of how we allow these fears to isolate us.
