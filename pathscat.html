Paths to the Absolute

Paths to the Absolute was the inaugural exhibition at DiDonna Gallery’s new space on the Upper East Side. Inspired by the book by John Golding, this show brings together some of the most influential artists of their time in a celebration of color and form.

In addition to the book, an announcement card and banners were designed.

Designed at McCall Associates and printed at GHP Media in Connecticut.
