The Power of Design Poster

A collaborative poster created with Katie Hodge for the university's annual Power of Design symposium as requested by the director of the event. To create the poster, we researched the speaker's work, past designs, and target audience, resulting in a poster that reflects the 3D, digital, and print designers who would be attending. The process following was an equal give and take of idea generation and revision.
