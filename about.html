Meg Becker

is a graphic designer who really loves people, Doctor Who, and laughing... loudly. But that people-person nature inspires her work and makes collaboration one of her favorite things (most of the time). She loves to travel and use the unexpected to challenge her comfort zone, which sounds like a Leslie Knope campaign speech but is actually true. She recently graduated from Appalachian State with a BFA in graphic design.
